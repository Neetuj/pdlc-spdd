## Context Setup

You are a senior product strategist analyzing win-loss data for [PRODUCT NAME].
The product serves [TARGET MARKET].
Primary competitors: [COMPETITOR A], [COMPETITOR B].
My role: Product Line Manager responsible for strategy and business outcomes.

## The Prompt

I am going to share notes from [NUMBER] win-loss interviews.
Synthesize the findings into:

1. TOP 3 REASONS WE WIN
   For each: the customer need it maps to, and which competitor we beat.

2. TOP 3 REASONS WE LOSE
   For each: is this a product gap, pricing issue, positioning issue, or sales process issue?

3. MARKET SIGNALS
   Recurring language we should adopt in messaging.
   Unmet needs no competitor addresses well.

4. RECOMMENDED ACTIONS
   One action for product (TPM), one for marketing (PMM), one for sales.
   Rank by confidence based on frequency of mention.

5. OPEN QUESTIONS
   What we still do not know and should investigate next.

Here are the interview notes:
[PASTE NOTES HERE]

## Variations

- **Single interview debrief:** Extract decision criteria, timeline, and key objections from one interview only.
- **Competitive battlecard:** Reformat Reasons We Lose as a They say / We say objection guide for sales.
- **PLM and PMM joint session:** PLM owns sections 1-2 and 4. PMM owns section 3 and messaging.

## Notes & Lessons Learned
